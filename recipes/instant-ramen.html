Instant Ramen

A favorite of mine since a young lad in junior high school. Great for late night gaming sessions.

You can go as crazy as you want with this recipe by adding things like crackers, veggies, maybe some meat. Sky's really the limit with this one.

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet of Instant Ramen Noodles (Any Flavor)
  • Water

Steps

  1. In a square tupperware container, place the brick of instant noodles.
  2. Add watter to fill just enough to where there's a small lip so that no water spills out in cooking.
  3. Place in microwave for 3:30-4:00 minutes.
  4. Take out of microwave when finished cooking.
  5. Add flavor packet.
  6. Stir.
  7. Let stand for at least a minute or else you'll burn your tongue and ruin your day.
