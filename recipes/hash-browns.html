Hash Browns

A Staple in the Breakfast World!

Cook some good buttery, crispy, golden hashbrowns that win over any crowd!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound Russet baking potatoes
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Peel and grate the potatoes.
  2. Squeeze out the moisture.
  3. Cook the hash browns by heating 3 tablespoons of oil in a large frying pan on medium-high heat and wait til its shimmering but not smoking. Add potatoes, spreading evenly along the bottom of the pan. Sprinkle some salt and pepper on them taters.
  4. Flip the hash browns when they've fried to a golden brown.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
